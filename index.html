Welcome To Oryx Group Media

In an era where cultural and social informative knowledge dissemination has become a very important driving force to a sane and productive society, it has become relevant to build a platform that helps achieve sharing untold stories, attractions and ties between Qatar and Africa. The platform will help Africans know more about Qatar, and Qataris about Africa. We will unravel the informative contents that showcase the differences and similarities between Africa, Qatar and the Arabia culture, resources and people, through the power of unique narratives and storytelling. Studies have shown that Africa and the Arab world have several cultural similarities which are evident in languages, religion, heritage, fashion, food, etc. ORYX GROUP MEDIA, an arm of Oryx Group, a Qatar registered company with CR. No. 135454, will drive the social, cultural and economic awareness between both regions. The platform through our various programs will engage in conversations that showcase the rich cultural fabric of Qatar and Africa, whilst exploring the centuries of heritage that connect these two prominent communities, Oryx Group Media will highlight the promising future brimming with multiple opportunities for cross-cultural, social and economic collaboration between Qatar and Africa.

Our Vision

To be an intercultural bridge between Arabian culture, Khaleeji heritage, and African cultures and people, whilst fostering cultural diplomacy, social and economic values within the regions

About Oryx Group Media

Our primary objective is to improve the Africa – Arabia social, economic and cultural connections and advancing opportunities for social, cultural and economic exchanges between both regions. We shall plan and deliver our programs with impeccable quality that will;

  1. Showcase and promote Qatari-Arabian culture to the African audience
  2. Showcase and promote African culture to the Qatari-Arabian audience
  3. Grow the Africa – Arabia connections in social, economic and cultural spheres
  4. Promote Tourism
  5. Become a social, cultural and economic informative hub
